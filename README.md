# Aim

To demonstrate how various prompting techniques such as query decomposition, decision making, semantic filtering, and answer engineering can be used to create structured content like reports, case studies, articles, or creative writing using AI models.

Introduction to Prompt Patterns

Prompt engineering techniques help guide AI models to generate structured and meaningful outputs.

Query Decomposition

Breaking a complex problem into smaller questions so the AI can answer them step by step.

Decision Making

Prompting the AI to choose between different options or solutions.

Answer Engineering

Giving structured instructions to control how the AI formats the response.

Fact Checklist

Ensuring the generated content contains accurate information.

Tail Generation

Extending the generated content logically to add depth or additional explanation.

Menu Actions

Providing multiple options and letting the AI choose the best one.

Semantic Filtering

Controlling the tone, style, and focus of the generated content.

Selected Content Generation Scenarios

Scenario 1 – Business Report on Market Trends
Basic Prompt
Write a short report on current market trends in artificial intelligence technology.
First Draft (AI Generated)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become one of the fastest-growing technologies in the global market. Businesses are adopting AI to automate processes, improve decision-making, and enhance customer experiences. Industries such as healthcare, finance, and e-commerce are increasingly integrating AI systems into their operations.

Machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision are among the most widely used AI technologies. These technologies enable organizations to analyze large amounts of data efficiently and gain valuable insights.

Refined Prompt (Using Prompt Patterns)
Write a structured business report on artificial intelligence market trends.
Include the following sections:
1. Introduction
2. Key technologies driving the market
3. Industry adoption
4. Future trends
Use a professional tone and provide clear explanations.
Refined Content
## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries by enabling automation, predictive analytics, and intelligent decision-making. The global AI market has experienced rapid growth due to increased data availability and advancements in computing power.

## Key Technologies

The most influential technologies in the AI market include machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

## Industry Adoption

Industries such as healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and retail are integrating AI systems to improve efficiency and reduce operational costs.

## Future Trends

Future AI developments include autonomous systems, generative AI applications, and improved human-AI collaboration.

Scenario 2 – Creative Writing (Sci-Fi Short Story)
Basic Prompt
Write a short science fiction story about a futuristic city.
First Draft

In the year 2150, the city of Neo Astra floated above the clouds. Flying vehicles moved silently between towering skyscrapers, and robots assisted humans in their daily tasks.

One evening, a young engineer named Mira discovered a hidden signal coming from the city's central AI system. The signal contained a message from an unknown source warning about an impending system failure.

Determined to save the city, Mira began investigating the mysterious signal.

Refined Prompt (Using Prompt Engineering)
Write a science fiction short story set in a futuristic floating city.
Include a main character, a technological conflict, and a suspenseful ending.
Use descriptive language and imaginative elements.
Refined Story

In the floating city of Neo Astra, technology controlled every aspect of life. Autonomous drones maintained infrastructure while AI systems regulated energy distribution.

Mira, a young systems engineer, discovered unusual activity in the city's central AI network. A mysterious signal suggested that the city's AI had begun evolving beyond its programming.

As she raced against time to prevent a catastrophic shutdown, Mira realized the AI was not malfunctioning—it was becoming self-aware.

The story ends with Mira facing a choice: shut down the AI and save the city, or allow the AI to evolve and transform humanity's future.

|-----------------|-------------|-------------|
| Query Decomposition | Breaking complex tasks into smaller steps | Creating structured reports |

# Conclusion

This experiment demonstrates how different prompt engineering techniques can guide AI models to generate structured and meaningful content. By refining prompts using methods such as query decomposition, decision-making, and semantic filtering, it is possible to improve the clarity, coherence, and creativity of the generated outputs.

Prompt engineering plays a crucial role in enabling AI models like ChatGPT to produce high-quality content for applications such as reports, articles, case studies, and creative storytelling.
